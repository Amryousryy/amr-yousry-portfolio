# STAGE 01 вҖ” ASSET EXPLORATION

## Purpose
The Memory Crystal stores compressed records of creative decisions. Each crystal holds a lesson, a pattern, or a successful approach. The Engine builds on itself through accumulated memory.

## Narrative Role
- The Engine's memory banks вҖ” every project, every decision, every lesson is preserved here
- Located in the Memory District (About Section)
- Passive вҖ” glows softly, responds to nothing
- Visual priority: medium вҖ” visible but not dominant
- Part of a system of independent storage units (crystals do not communicate with each other)

## Relationship to Surrounding Systems
- **Creative Core** feeds energy to crystals (narrative only вҖ” no visual energy transfer)
- **Other Memory Crystals** are independent вҖ” no inter-crystal communication
- **About Section text** вҖ” crystals are decorative accents near skill summaries, bio highlights, experience markers
- **Depth Layer:** L4 (Foreground Ambient) вҖ” close to the viewer

## Design Constraints
- Diamond/rhombus silhouette вҖ” 12px wide Г— 16px tall at native
- 16Г—16px canvas with 1px safe padding
- Must be readable at 50% scale (24Г—24px display)
- Must be visible against #1E1B4B background (2.5:1 minimum contrast)
- Palette: Deep Purple, Dark Indigo, Light Purple, Electric Cyan (4 colors only)
